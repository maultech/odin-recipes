Pepperoni Mushroom Pizza . . . How to prepare:

Ingredients

  • Pepperoni
  • Mushrooms
  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Grease the cooking surface you place pizza on
  2. Stretch Dough
  3. Add/Distribute sauce
  4. Layer cheese
  5. Add pepperoni and mushrooms
  6. Preheat oven to 200° 10 minutes
  7. Bake at 190°15-16 minutes
